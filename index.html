About Me

Researcher. Educator.
Course Architect.

I hold a Doctor of Philosophy in Combustion and Fire Safety from the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi — one of India's premier research institutions. With over eight years of professional experience, my work spans experimental combustion research, mathematical modeling, CFD simulation, and higher education.

Currently, I serve as an Instructor and Content Area Lead at the University of the People, where I deliver courses in Algebra, Calculus, Discrete Mathematics, Statistics, and Probability. I review course materials for accessibility standards and contribute to inclusive design in online learning platforms.

I am deeply committed to expanding female participation in STEM disciplines and actively participate in departmental initiatives that create an inclusive academic environment for women in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.
